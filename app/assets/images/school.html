Frequently Asked Questions

What curriculum does the school provide?

We run the National British curriculum with a blend of the Nigerian curriculum

Are new pupils required to write any entrance test?

Yes. Pupils coming into Year 1 - Year 6 are required to write an entrance test in English and Mathematics.

Do you provide lunch?

Yes, we provide healthy, nutritious cooked meals for our children. These meals are eaten in a clean, hygienic and expansive dining hall.

Is the School Lunch already a part of the bill?

No. The School Lunch is an optional part of the bill. Parents can decided to waive it or go for it.

Do you provide bus services?

Yes, we provide school bus services to selected routes. Bowine does not own these buses but we monitor, control and scrutinize the bus service providers every school day.

What type of sports does the school offer?

We offer both indoor and outdoor sports. These include football, basketball, lawn tennis, swimming, athletics, taekwando, chess, ballet and more

How do I get the application form?

You can purchase the form by walking into our school or from our website.

Does the school use age of child to place them in their appropriate class levels?

Yes we do. Your child has to be a certain age by the 1st of September to be eligible for admission. For example, a child has to be at least 3 years old for admission into Pre-nursery in Preschool and 6 years old for admission into Year 2 in Elementary School.

What educational service does Bowine International School provide?

We provide Pre-school (18 months – 4 years), Elementary School (5years – 10years) and A-Star Academy (for pupils preparing for entrance examinations)

Do you provide after school care?

Yes, we offer after school care based on request. Please contact the school for more information
